# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: December 9, 2025  
**Last Updated**: December 9, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to pNode Pulse ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our pNode network analytics platform.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Wallet Address** (Required for Authentication)
- Your Solana wallet public key (Base58 encoded)
- Used for wallet-based authentication
- Not considered personally identifiable information (public blockchain data)

**Email Address** (Optional)
- Provided voluntarily for alert notifications
- Used only if you configure email notification channels
- Can be removed at any time from your account settings

**Display Name** (Optional)
- Custom username for your operator profile
- Publicly visible if you choose to make your profile public

### 2. Information We Collect Automatically

**Session Data**
- IP Address (for security and rate limiting)
- User Agent (browser and device information)
- Login timestamp
- Last activity timestamp
- Session duration

**Usage Data**
- Pages visited
- Features used
- API requests made
- Timestamp of interactions

**Performance Data**
- Page load times
- Error logs (anonymized)
- Browser console errors

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Private keys or seed phrases (never requested or stored)
- ❌ Transaction history from your wallet
- ❌ Personal identifying information (name, address, phone)
- ❌ Financial information (credit cards, bank accounts)
- ❌ Precise geolocation data

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Authentication & Account Management
- Verify wallet ownership via cryptographic signatures
- Maintain your login session (7-day expiration)
- Associate your data (node claims, alerts, portfolios) with your account

### Service Functionality
- Send alert notifications you've configured
- Generate performance reports you've scheduled
- Display your operator profile (if public)
- Track API usage for rate limiting

### Security & Abuse Prevention
- Detect and prevent unauthorized access
- Enforce rate limits to prevent API abuse
- Identify and block malicious activity
- Maintain audit logs for security incidents

### Analytics & Improvements
- Understand how users interact with the platform
- Identify and fix bugs
- Improve user experience and performance
- Plan new features based on usage patterns

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| **User Account** | Until account deletion requested | Core functionality |
| **Session Data** | 7 days after last login | Security audit trail |
| **Node Metrics** | 90 days rolling window | Platform analytics |
| **Audit Logs** | 180 days | Security compliance |
| **Email Notifications** | No storage (sent immediately) | Privacy by design |

### Account Deletion

You can request full account deletion at any time:
- All personally identifiable data deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized analytics data may be retained (no connection to your wallet)
- Public blockchain data (wallet address) cannot be deleted

## Your Privacy Rights

### Under GDPR (European Users)

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object**: Object to processing of your data
- **Restrict**: Limit how we use your data

### Under CCPA (California Users)

You have the right to:
- **Know**: What personal information we collect and how it's used
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Opt-Out**: Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### How to Exercise Your Rights

Email us at **privacy@rectorspace.com** with:
- Your wallet address (for verification)
- Specific request (access, deletion, correction, etc.)
- We will respond within 30 days

## Cookies & Tracking

### Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies** (Required):
- Authentication session cookie
- CSRF protection token
- Lasts 7 days, HTTP-only, secure flag

**Analytics Cookies** (Optional, if enabled):
- Error tracking (Sentry)
- Performance monitoring
- Anonymized usage statistics

### Cookie Consent

By using pNode Pulse, you consent to our use of essential cookies for authentication and security. You can opt out of analytics cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

### Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals. If DNT is enabled, we will not use analytics cookies.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

**Technical Safeguards**:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted data at rest (database encryption)
- Secure authentication (cryptographic signatures)
- Rate limiting and DDoS protection
- Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning

**Organizational Safeguards**:
- Access controls (principle of least privilege)
- Regular security training for team members
- Incident response plan
- Data breach notification procedures

**Database Backups**:
- Daily encrypted backups with 30-day retention
- Access logs for all backup operations
- Secure deletion of expired backups
